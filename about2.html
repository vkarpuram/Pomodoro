Description

Pomodoro is a web based time management platform that allows the user to schedule daily tasks, keeps track of their distractions during an ongoing task and helps them concentrate on their tasks by providing an environment where they can be productive with tools implemented in the app. The app manages the userâ€™s time utilizing the Pomodoro technique, schedules tasks and tracks their progress. The user will learn how to plan their events more efficiently, be more productive and make positive changes to their lives.

Pomodoro Techique

Pomodoro is a time management method, in which user will break down a task into 4 cycles of 25 minutes each, and cycles have 5 minute breaks between them. After 4 cycles, user will take 30 minute break.
